EXAMINATION

Engineering Entrance Examinations 2018 Students can appear in various national, state & university level examinations to get specialized branch like CSE, IT, Civil, Mechanical, Petroleum, Electronics & Communication, etc. Some of the famous engineering entrance exams are JEE Main, JEE Advanced, GATE, IIT JAM, etc




NATIONAL LEVEL

JEE Main 2018 JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) Main is a national level examination. It is managed by Central Board Secondary Education (CBSE).

GATE 2018 GATE referred as Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering. It is a national level engineering entrance exam organized by IITs & IISC rotationally

ICAR AIEEA 2018 Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) conducts the AIEEA (All India Entrance Examination for Admission) examination.




UNVERSITY LEVEL

KCET 2018 KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test) is a state level exam organized by Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA).

Punjab JET 2018 Punjab State Board of Technical Education & Industrial Training, Chandigarh (PSBTE & IT) is the authority which conducts Punjab JET

UPSEE 2018 UPSEE (Uttar Pradesh State Entrance Examination) also referred as UPTU is a state level entrance exam. This exam is organized by Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam Technical University, Uttar Pradesh.
